# App Store Positioning Kit

Positioning line (everywhere): **Find your food triggers. Watch your Gut Score climb.**

Hard rules baked into product decisions (do not violate in any store asset):
- No unverifiable trust claims (star ratings, user counts) until real ones exist.
- No medical-certainty or "heal/fix/cure" language. "Identify suspected triggers," never "diagnose."
- No program/phase framing (learn→calm→reintroduce is not marketed). Two pillars only: Gut Score + Trigger Profile.
- Detective vocabulary (suspects, confirmed, cleared) appears only when showing the Triggers screen itself.

## Title / subtitle (30-char limits)

- **Title:** `MyTummyHurts: Food Triggers` (27)
- **Subtitle:** `Scan menus. Spot triggers.` (26)
- Alternates to A/B: `Gut Check Food & Menu Scanner` / `IBS & reflux food scanner`

## Keyword field (100 chars)

`ibs,gerd,acid reflux,bloating,food triggers,gut health,fodmap,food scanner,menu,sensitivity,diary`

## Description (lead paragraphs)

> **Know how a meal is likely to treat your gut — before you eat it.**
>
> Scan any restaurant menu and MyTummyHurts ranks every dish against *your* gut — your conditions (IBS, reflux, lactose intolerance), your sensitivities, and what your body has actually shown over time. No more menu anxiety. No more guessing.
>
> **Your Trigger Profile, built automatically.** Tell us what you suspect, then live your life. One 10-second check-in a day — a single tap on a notification — and the app confirms or clears each suspected trigger with real evidence. No elimination diet. No food diary homework.
>
> **A Gut Score that's honest.** One number for how your gut is trending, driven by your actual days — not wellness vibes.

Feature bullets:
- Menu scanning: every dish ranked for your gut, with the *why* ("Because you told us: dairy + reflux")
- Meal photo & barcode scanning with personalized risk and safer swaps
- Trigger Profile: suspects → confirmed or cleared, with confidence and evidence counts
- One-tap daily check-ins (Calm / Meh / Rough) from the notification
- Weekly gut report: score movement, calm days, trigger evidence
- Built on published gut-trigger research; informational guidance, not medical advice
